Product photos for the fertilizer store (50kg Hayleys bags):

  urea.png  — Urea
  tsp.png   — Triple Super Phosphate (TSP)
  mop.png   — Muriate of Potash (MOP)

Place those three PNG files in this folder. They are served at /fertilizer-products/urea.png etc.

If you attached images in Cursor, run from the project root:
  powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File scripts/copy-fertilizer-images.ps1
